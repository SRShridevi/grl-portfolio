SHRI'S ALL TIME FAVOS

My fav 3 movies of all time

MS DHONI THE UNTOLD STORY

This inspiring movie depicts the life of Mahendra Singh Dhoni,my fav cricketer. The way they showed all the struggles that MS had in his initial stage of learning cricket was very motivational.This is the best movie for all the youngsters to follow their aspiration. The journey of Mahi from a small town to Captain of Team India gave really goosebumps. As a true fan of him the obsession that i have towards him and this movie is relentlessly high.

2.NANBAN

This humorous film is primarily about the friendship that bloomed in college,the challenges they face, and their fun interactions. "Nanban" delivers a valuable message about the need for holistic education, the importance of pursuing one's dreams, and the significance of true friendship. Overall this a good movie which has plenty of good things.

3.CHENNAI EXPRESS

"Chennai Express" is a light-hearted and entertaining Bollywood film that combines humor, action, and romance. It features strong performances by its lead actors, catchy music, and a positive message about cultural unity.The chemistry between the Rahul and Meenamma is uncomparable.The film's success and enduring popularity make it a significant addition to Indian cinema.
